About Me





Every day I am amazed by the possibilities available to us through programming. With a background in graphic design and an insatiable need to draw, I have an eye for detail that translates to my code.

I am an eager learner and a constant perfectionist; I love problem-solving challenges and beautiful design.

When I'm not in front of a computer I can be found riding my horses or running alongside my donkey.
